Who We Are

Pioneering Smart Living Solutions

Pedro Ourique is a forward-thinking initiative dedicated to exploring the practical applications and community desire for home automation technologies.

Our core mission is to bridge the gap between innovative smart home concepts and the everyday needs of homeowners. We believe that technology should enhance life, making it simpler, more secure, and more enjoyable. Through comprehensive field research, we aim to gather insights that will shape the future of residential automation, ensuring it's both accessible and truly beneficial.

We're not just about gadgets; we're about creating intelligent environments that adapt to you.

Understanding Your Smart Home Needs

Our field research focuses on identifying which home automation features resonate most with potential users. Your feedback helps us understand priorities for convenience, security, energy efficiency, and overall lifestyle enhancement.

Intelligent Lighting

Automated scheduling, voice control, and mood lighting. How important is effortless ambiance and energy savings through smart lights?

Smart Climate Control

Learning thermostats, zone control, and remote adjustments. What value do you place on optimized comfort and reduced energy bills?

Enhanced Security

Smart locks, AI-powered cameras, and integrated alarm systems. How critical is advanced, accessible home security for your peace of mind?

Seamless Entertainment

Voice-activated media, multi-room audio, and integrated home theaters. How appealing is a fully connected and intuitive entertainment setup?
